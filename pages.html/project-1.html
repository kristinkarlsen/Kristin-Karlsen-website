WEBSITE PROJECT FOR Suave Salong



Suave salong is a hair salon in Oslo with experienced and talented hairdressers. Suave wanted a more visually appealing website design in line with their rebranding and new logo. The finished design suits their brand, attracts their target audience, and has a sensual and romantic vibe. Customers may easily schedule appointments and locate any information they may want. To apply to be employed as a hairdresser in their salon, you can simply fill out a form on their website with your resume. For this project I also produced the text and collected images from their Instagram and Adobe Stock.
