WEBSITE PROJECT FOR LJOMA HUD



For this job, I utilised WordPress and Elementor to build a website for a skin clinic in Oslo, Norway using a combination of templates and my own design. Ljoma Hud is a high quality clinic with many loyal customers. I have been working with them since they opened their clinic in the start of 2022. The owner wanted something different and colorful, but still elegantly clean. This design is strongly thought of as a part of building their brand. Customers may easily schedule appointments and access desired information about treatments and the clinic. Along with managing the clinic's website, I also assisted with some marketing strategy and social media promotion.
