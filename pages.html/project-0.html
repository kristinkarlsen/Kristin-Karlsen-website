WEBSITE PROJECT FOR JUST PEACHY



I used a combination of templates and my own design to develop a visually appealing website for Just Peachy using WordPress and Elementor. Lashes, brows, and waxing are the areas of expertise at Just Peachy, a high-end beauty salon in Bergen. They want a simple, trendy website that reflects their identity and appeals to their intended audience. Customers are easily able to find the information they need and make appointments.
